Information We Collect

We collect details you provide when joining our mailing list or contacting us. Analytics tools also gather general usage data to improve the site.

  • Required: Email address and interest selections for lead magnet delivery.
  • Optional: Buying goal, preferred channel, or custom message so concierge outreach is relevant.
  • Automatic: Anonymized device data, aggregated page metrics, and referrer URLs.
How We Use Information

Information is used to send updates, respond to inquiries, and enhance site performance. Cookies may be stored on your device to support these functions, and we do not sell or share your information with third parties except as required by law.

Segmentation selections influence which Drop Kit magnet you receive and which lifecycle emails trigger after purchase. We never use sensitive data categories.

Cookie Usage

We use essential and analytics cookies to remember your preferences and understand site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Analytics Tools

We use privacy-focused analytics tools to measure visits and performance. These tools collect aggregated data and store it for up to 24 months.

Key metrics tracked: page engagement, Drop Kit form completion rate, and loyalty program clicks. IP addresses are truncated and never stored alongside personal data.

Communications Lifecycle

We schedule lifecycle messaging to match the post-purchase roadmap:

  1. Instant: Welcome email with your selected lead magnet and data usage reminder.
  2. Within 24 hours: Concierge follow-up if you asked a question or booked a meetup.
  3. Post-purchase: Order confirmation, packaging photos, delivery check-in, and loyalty summary.
  4. Ongoing: One weekly digest with marketplace highlights unless you opt out.

Text messages and Discord updates are strictly opt-in and can be disabled by replying STOP or updating your preferences.

Data Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Data may be shared with service providers that help operate the site or as required by law.

Vendors with access: Netlify (hosting/forms), Mailchimp (email segmentation), and privacy-focused analytics. Each provider signs a data processing agreement.

Data Security

We protect your information using secure transport (HTTPS), limited admin access, and encryption-at-rest policies provided by our vendors.

  • Lead magnet files are stored on Netlify with signed URLs.
  • Access to subscriber tags is limited to Hector and one verified contractor.
  • Security reviews are performed quarterly and after any major feature change.
Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of analytics by blocking cookies or using browser extensions. To stop receiving emails or remove your information, use unsubscribe links or contact us.

To adjust segmentation preferences, resubmit the Drop Kit form or reply to any email with your new interests.

Data Retention

Mailing list data is kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Aggregated analytics data is retained for up to 24 months to help improve the site.

Inactive subscribers (no opens for 180 days) are automatically removed from segmented campaigns to minimize data exposure.
