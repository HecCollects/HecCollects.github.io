About HecCollects

Hey! I’m Hector – collector, curator, and all‑around hobby nerd. From childhood nostalgia to investment‑grade assets, I've always looked for the value in products and how they compare within their respective hobbies. I price just under market online (with bigger breaks in person) to keep the hobby fun and accessible. Let’s share the passion!
