Returns Policy

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery. Sealed products must arrive with factory wrap intact and in original condition. Buyers pay return shipping unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.

Initiating a Return

  1. Email hectorsandoval1402@gmail.com or use the contact form with your order number, photos, and reason for return.
  2. Receive approval and instructions within 24 hours, including a prepaid label if the return is due to damage or misdescription.
  3. Drop the package within 5 business days to keep eligibility active.

Packaging Checklist

Use this checklist to keep your sealed return eligible:

  • Include all sealed items, promo packs, and COAs.
  • Keep the shrink wrap untouched, place the box back in the original team bag or bubble wrap, and support corners with the provided foam.
  • Seal with tamper-evident tape or include photos of your sealing method for authenticity review.
  • Place the RMA slip (provided upon approval) on top for faster inspection.

Need packing materials? Request a return kit with fresh tamper tape and corner protectors through the concierge channel.

Refund Exceptions

Custom or personalized orders and digital items are non-refundable unless defective. Shipping costs are non-refundable once an order has shipped.
