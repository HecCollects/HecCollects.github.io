Orders & Shipping

Orders are double-boxed or shipper-protected within 2 business days. Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on the carrier and destination, and every sealed Pokémon product travels with tamper seals and corner guards.

  • Tracking cadence: You receive an order confirmation instantly, a prep photo set within 12 hours, and carrier tracking once scanned.
  • Shipping upgrades: Loyalty Pro and Elite tiers receive one free rush label per quarter—reply to the confirmation email if you need case-level insurance or temperature control.
  • Local pickup: Reserve sealed inventory through the OfferUp Fast Pass, then schedule a meetup window with Hector to verify wrap seams under UV light.
Returns & Refunds

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery if the seal remains intact and items are in original condition. Custom or digital items are non-returnable unless defective, and refunds are issued to the original payment method once items are received.

Start a return by emailing hectorsandoval1402@gmail.com with your order number and photos. You'll receive a prepaid label when the issue is on us, or DIY instructions if the return is preference-based.

For a step-by-step guide, visit the Shipping & Returns page.

Authenticity & Grading

Each sealed drop is authenticated using a multi-step protocol:

  1. Visual inspection: 10x magnification check for crimp patterns, corner dents, or seal tampering.
  2. Cross-reference: Case codes and certification numbers are logged against Pokémon Center and PSA/BGS/CGC databases.
  3. Documentation: Photo gallery, weight logs, and grading notes shared via your order confirmation.

Want a professional grading pre-screen? Add the request in checkout notes and I'll include the PSA prep sheet featured in our Certification Badges section.
